## v1.0 - IPRS Portfolio Analytics Platform

**Shipped:** 2026-02-12
**Phases:** 4 (25 plans)
**Timeline:** 11 days (2026-02-01 to 2026-02-12)
**Commits:** 106
**Lines Added:** ~46,753 (17,195 Python, 2,014 TSX, 707 SQL, 1,200 Terraform)

**Delivered:** Institutional-grade portfolio analytics platform with 8 pricers, full risk analytics (market/credit/liquidity), portfolio management with concentration monitoring, regulatory compliance (CECL/Basel/GAAP-IFRS), and 8-page React frontend with export and alerting.

**Key Accomplishments:**
1. Institutional-grade pricing engine with 8 pricers, QuantLib curve construction, and 90+ golden tests
2. Full risk analytics suite: market risk (duration, DV01, convexity), credit risk (PD, EL, UL, RAROC), liquidity risk (LCR), VaR/ES
3. Portfolio management with hierarchy trees, position tracking, multi-currency FX, content-addressable snapshots
4. Regulatory compliance: CECL allowance (ASC 326), Basel III RWA, GAAP/IFRS valuation, immutable audit trails
5. Data ingestion pipelines for market feeds, loan servicing, and reference data with lineage tracking
6. React frontend with 8 interactive pages and CSV/Excel export
